Cooking

I got into cooking and trying to make new recipes for myself a few years ago as I'd ended up working a shift on Christmas day. As it would've been a waste to have a whole turkey to myself, I'd looked up to how to properly cook and butter-baste a steak and made some chips along side it. All in all it was quite simple but from there I'd slowly learnt how to make more recipes such as chicken fried rice, fish soups, different types of sauces as well as a variety of cooking techniques. Nowadays, I do most of the cooking for myself and my partner and it's very satisfying being able to make something for someone else that they really enjoy.
